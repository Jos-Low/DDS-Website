Hood Cleaning FAQ

What is commercial kitchen hood cleaning?

Hood cleaning removes grease and residue from kitchen exhaust hoods, ducts, and fans to reduce fire risk and meet NFPA 96 and health department standards.

How often should a commercial kitchen hood be cleaned?

NFPA 96 recommends monthly for high-volume kitchens, quarterly for moderate use, and semi-annually or annually for low-volume operations. Our techs recommend the right schedule after your free inspection.

Do you clean outside of business hours?

Yes — always. We schedule overnight and early-morning appointments so your kitchen has zero downtime during service hours.

What is NFPA 96 and why does it matter?

NFPA 96 is the National Fire Protection Association's standard for commercial kitchen ventilation and fire protection. Compliance is required by most local fire codes and can affect your insurance. We provide full documentation after every clean.

What areas of Michigan do you serve?

We serve restaurants and commercial kitchens throughout Michigan — Lansing, Detroit, Grand Rapids, Flint, Kalamazoo, and surrounding areas.

Are your technicians certified and insured?

Yes. All technicians are NFPA 96 trained and certified, and we carry full liability insurance on every job.
