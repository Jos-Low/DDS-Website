Exterior Cleaning

House & Building Washing Services

Safe, Thorough Cleaning That Protects Your Property

Mold, mildew, algae, and years of Michigan weather leave their mark on every exterior. We use the right pressure and the right chemistry to restore your home or building — without risking damage to siding, trim, or landscaping.

Restore Your Home's Exterior & Protect Its Value

A dirty exterior isn't just an eyesore — mold and algae can degrade siding, clog drainage, and signal to buyers or guests that a property isn't well-maintained. Regular professional washing preserves materials, improves curb appeal, and keeps your investment in top shape.

The Right Method for Every Surface

Soft Wash & Pressure Wash — We Match the Technique to the Material

Recommended for most surfaces

Soft Washing

Low pressure combined with biodegradable cleaning solutions breaks down organic growth at the source — killing mold, algae, and mildew rather than just blasting it off.

  • Vinyl, wood & fiber cement siding
  • Stucco & EIFS
  • Painted surfaces
  • Soffits, fascia & trim
  • Cedar shingles & wood panels
For durable surfaces

Pressure Washing

High-pressure water removes heavy grime, paint prep residue, and stubborn deposits from hard surfaces that can handle the force without damage.

  • Brick & block
  • Concrete foundations
  • Metal siding & panels
  • Commercial building exteriors
  • Parking garages & loading docks

Surfaces We Clean

Residential & Commercial Exteriors — All Siding Types

Most Popular

Vinyl Siding

Soft-wash treatment removes black streaking, green algae, and oxidation — restoring the original color without warping or cracking.

Brick & Masonry

Low-pressure soft wash prevents moisture intrusion and removes algae from stucco's textured surface safely — no cracking or spalling.

Wood & Hardiboard

Gentle cleaning for natural wood and fiber cement siding that removes buildup without raising grain or damaging factory finishes.

Metal Panels

Remove oxidation, bird droppings, and industrial grime from aluminum and steel siding used on commercial and agricultural buildings.

Multi-Story & Commercial

Reach and clean office buildings, retail storefronts, apartment complexes, and warehouses — safely and efficiently.

Our House Washing Process

Careful, Thorough & Respectful of Your Property

  1. Pre-Service Assessment

    We walk the exterior, identify surface types, and note any sensitive areas — windows, plants, fixtures — that need protection.

  2. Protect the Surroundings

    Landscaping is pre-wetted. Sensitive fixtures, vents, and electrical components are covered as needed.

  3. Apply Cleaning Solution

    Biodegradable detergent is applied to dwell and break down organic growth, oxidation, and surface grime.

  4. Wash & Rinse

    Surface is rinsed with the appropriate pressure — low for delicate materials, higher for masonry and concrete — from top to bottom.

  5. Final Walk-Through

    We inspect with you to confirm every area meets our standard before we pack up and leave.

House Washing FAQ

Is soft washing safe for all types of siding?

Yes. Soft washing is designed for delicate surfaces and is actually safer than high-pressure washing for vinyl, wood, stucco, and painted exteriors. We adjust the cleaning solution and pressure to match each surface type.

Will the cleaning solution harm my plants and grass?

We use biodegradable, plant-safe detergents and pre-wet all landscaping before work begins. After washing, we rinse surrounding vegetation again. We take great care to protect your yard.

How long does house washing take?

Most residential homes take 2–4 hours depending on size and level of buildup. Larger commercial buildings or heavily soiled properties may take longer. We'll give you a time estimate with your quote.

How often should a house be washed?

Every 1–2 years is typical for Michigan homes, though properties near trees, bodies of water, or in shadier areas may accumulate algae faster and benefit from annual cleaning.

Do you wash commercial buildings too?

Absolutely. We work with property managers, HOAs, retail centers, office buildings, and industrial facilities of all sizes. We also offer recurring maintenance plans for commercial clients.

Do I need to be home during the service?

Not necessarily — as long as we have access to an outdoor water spigot and know which areas need attention. Many customers just leave instructions and return to a freshly cleaned home.
