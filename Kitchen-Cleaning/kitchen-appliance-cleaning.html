Appliance Cleaning FAQ

How often should appliances be deep cleaned?

Most commercial kitchens benefit from professional deep cleaning every 3–6 months, with high-volume concepts on the shorter end.

Can dirty appliances cause health code issues?

Yes. Grease and residue can contribute to failed inspections. Scheduled deep cleaning reduces that risk.

Do you clean during off-hours?

Yes. Overnight and early-morning service is available to avoid disrupting service.

Are your products food-safe?

We use food-safe sanitizers and degreasers appropriate for commercial kitchens, including NSF-listed products where required.
