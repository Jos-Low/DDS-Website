Filter Cleaning FAQ

How often should filters be cleaned or replaced?

Many high-volume kitchens clean filters every 2–4 weeks and replace media every 3–6 months. Your ideal schedule depends on menu, volume, and local requirements.

Can I clean filters myself?

Rinsing helps between services, but deep grease removal usually needs professional degreasing and pressure washing to address fire risk fully.

Do you offer scheduled exchange programs?

Yes. We build replacement schedules around your volume so you’re never running on saturated filters.

Are replacement filters NFPA 96 compliant?

We install filters appropriate for commercial kitchen exhaust and fire safety expectations for your system.
