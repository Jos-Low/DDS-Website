Kitchen Cleaning

Hood System Cleaning Services

Professional Hood Cleaning for Safer, Compliant Kitchens

Grease buildup in commercial hoods is a major fire hazard and an NFPA 96 concern. We remove grease, restore airflow, and document every job so you’re ready for fire marshals and health inspectors.

NFPA-Compliant Cleaning to Reduce Fire Risk & Improve Ventilation

Our certified technicians use specialized degreasers, pressure washing, and proven methods to clean your entire exhaust path — not just what you can see from the line. That means safer staff, better air movement, and fewer surprises at inspection time.

Why Choose Dirty Deeds Hood System Cleaning?

Fire Safety, Compliance & Peak Kitchen Efficiency

Regular hood cleaning protects people, property, and your brand. We help you pass inspections, improve air quality, and keep the kitchen running at full output.

  • Reduces Fire Hazards

    Removes grease and flammable buildup that fuel kitchen exhaust fires.

  • NFPA 96 Compliance

Hood System FAQ

How often should I schedule hood cleaning?

High-volume kitchens often need cleaning every 3 months; lower-volume operations may qualify for semi-annual service. We recommend a schedule after your first assessment.

Will I receive proof of compliance?

Yes. You get a detailed post-cleaning report and service documentation suitable for fire and health inspectors.

Do you clean rooftop grease containment systems?

Yes. We maintain and service rooftop grease containment to help protect your roof and stay aligned with NFPA expectations.

Do you provide after-hours cleaning?

Absolutely. We schedule overnight and early-morning service to minimize disruption to your operation.
