Exterior Cleaning FAQ

What's the difference between pressure washing and soft washing?

Pressure washing uses high-force water to blast away grime — ideal for concrete and brick. Soft washing uses low pressure with specialized cleaning solutions to safely treat delicate surfaces like siding, roofing, and wood without causing damage.

Will exterior cleaning damage my landscaping?

We take precautions to protect your plants and lawn. Our cleaning solutions are biodegradable and plant-safe, and we rinse surrounding vegetation before and after work.

How often should I have my exterior cleaned?

Most homes benefit from a full exterior wash every 1–2 years, with driveways and gutters potentially needing attention more frequently. We can recommend a maintenance schedule after your first visit.

Do you serve commercial properties?

Yes. We work with homeowners, property managers, HOAs, retail centers, warehouses, and more. Multi-location programs are available.

What areas of Michigan do you serve?

We serve communities throughout Michigan including Lansing, East Lansing, Grand Rapids, Ann Arbor, Detroit, Flint, Kalamazoo, and surrounding areas. See our full service area.
