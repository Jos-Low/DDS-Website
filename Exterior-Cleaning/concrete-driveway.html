Concrete Cleaning FAQ

Will pressure washing damage my concrete?

No. We use commercial surface cleaners with the correct PSI to clean effectively without etching or harming your surfaces. Consumer-grade washers held too close can damage concrete — our equipment and technique prevent that.

How often should I clean my driveway or parking lot?

At least once a year maintains cleanliness and prevents deep-set stains. High-traffic commercial areas — parking lots, loading docks — often benefit from semi-annual service.

Can you remove oil stains from concrete?

Yes. While some very deep stains require multiple treatments, our pre-treatment process and high-temperature washing lifts most oil and automotive stains effectively.

Are your cleaning solutions safe for plants and grass?

We use biodegradable, eco-friendly solutions that break down stains without harming nearby landscaping. We also rinse surrounding areas as needed.

Do you serve both residential and commercial properties?

Absolutely. We clean residential driveways, commercial parking lots, warehouse floors, loading docks, and any other hard surface that needs attention across Michigan.
