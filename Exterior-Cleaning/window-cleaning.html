Our Window Cleaning Process

Methodical, Thorough, Streak-Free

  1. Pre-Inspection

    We assess window condition, frame type, and any problem areas like hard-water staining or oxidation before we start.

  2. Scrubbing & Agitation

    Cleaning solution is applied and scrubbed in to loosen grime, bug debris, bird droppings, and deposits.

  3. Squeegee & Detail Work

    Professional-grade squeegees remove solution cleanly, with hand-detailing on frames, sills, and corners.

  4. Final Quality Check

    Every pane is inspected before we leave — no streaks, no water spots, no missed spots.

Window Cleaning FAQ

How often should windows be professionally cleaned?

Most homes benefit from professional cleaning twice a year. Commercial storefronts with high foot traffic often need monthly or quarterly service to maintain a professional appearance year-round.

Do you clean interior and exterior windows?

Yes. We can clean exterior windows only, or both interior and exterior — just let us know what you need when you request your quote.

Can you remove hard-water stains from windows?

Yes, in most cases. We use specialized treatments to break down mineral deposits. Very old or severe staining may require multiple treatments or specialized restoration products.

Is your cleaning solution safe for my plants and pets?

Absolutely. We use eco-friendly, biodegradable solutions that are safe for people, pets, and surrounding landscaping.

Do you clean windows on multi-story buildings?

Yes. We have the equipment and safety protocols to handle multi-story residential and commercial window cleaning safely and efficiently.
