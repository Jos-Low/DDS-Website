Gutter Cleaning FAQ

How often should gutters be cleaned in Michigan?

Twice a year is the standard recommendation — once in late spring after seed and pollen season, and once in late fall after leaves have dropped. If you have many overhanging trees, quarterly service may make sense.

Can clogged gutters cause foundation damage?

Yes. When gutters overflow, water pools against your foundation and can cause cracks, basement leaks, and structural settling over time. Regular cleaning is one of the most cost-effective ways to protect your home.

Do you clean gutters on multi-story buildings?

Yes. We have the equipment and experience to safely service gutters on multi-story residential and commercial properties.

Will you remove and reinstall gutter guards?

We can clean around most gutter guard systems. If removal is necessary, we discuss that with you before starting work.

Do you offer ongoing gutter maintenance plans?

Yes. We can set up a scheduled program — bi-annual or quarterly — so your gutters are always clear and you never have to think about it.
