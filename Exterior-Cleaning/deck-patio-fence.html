Our Deck, Patio & Fence Cleaning Process

The Right Method for Every Material

  1. Material Assessment

  2. Pre-Treatment

    Biodegradable cleaning agents are applied to break down organic growth, stains, and embedded dirt.

Deck & Patio Cleaning FAQ

Can pressure washing damage my wood deck?

It can, if done incorrectly. We use the proper nozzle, pressure setting, and distance for each wood type. For older or softer wood, we often use soft washing with a low-pressure rinse to prevent any damage.

How often should I clean my deck or patio?

Annually is a good baseline in Michigan. Decks with heavy shade or near trees may benefit from cleaning twice a year due to faster mold and algae growth in damp conditions.

Can you prep my deck for staining or sealing?

Yes — and we recommend it. A clean, properly prepared surface accepts stain and sealer far better than a dirty or mildewy one, extending the life of your finish significantly.

Do you clean vinyl and aluminum fences?

Yes. Vinyl and aluminum fences respond very well to soft washing — it removes oxidation, algae, and dirt without scratching or fading the surface.

Will you clean around my plants and landscaping?

Yes. We take care to protect surrounding plantings, rinse off any overspray, and use biodegradable solutions to minimize any impact on your landscaping.
